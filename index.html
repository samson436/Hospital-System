Matuu Mission Dispensary is a Faith Based Organization located in Yatta Distric, Eastern Province. It is managed by Sisters of Our Lady of the Missions. The dispensary commenced its operation in the year 1997. The initial aim was to help the poor/underprivileged people in Yatta Plateau. To accomplish this, it begun with preventive and curative treatment besides outreach clinics. Following the example of Jesus, the healer, Sisters of Our Lady of the Missions are committed to promote life for all through having a listening heart and giving compassionate care. Staff members are committed to uphold high professional standards, respect confidentiality and work in collaboration with other health providers. At present the facility provides accessible, quality, affordable services –preventive and curatives to all clients regardless of their status.

SERVICES OFFERED

Outpatient Services

Examples of outpatient services include doctor visits, diagnostic tests, laboratory tests, physical therapy, occupational therapy, speech therapy, and minor surgical procedures. We aslo offer services such as vaccinations, immunizations, and screenings, as well as chronic care for patients with ongoing medical conditions.

Antenatal and Postnatal Services

Antenatal services offered include prenatal care, ultrasound, genetic testing, and nutrition counseling. We also have child Welfare Clinic 24 hours maternity services and Cervical cancer screening. Our postnatal services include postpartum care, breastfeeding support, newborn screening, and mental health counseling.

Dental clinic-Every Tuesday

Our dental clinic offers comprehensive and specialized dental services. It is staffed by dental professionals who are trained and experienced in providing a wide range of dental services, including preventive and restorative dentistry, cosmetic dentistry, orthodontics, and oral surgery

CCC Clinic

We offer TB and HIV testing and MGT for free. The clinic offers testing for both diseases as well as management and treatment options. The clinic is typically staffed by medical professionals who are qualified to help individuals understand their risks and provide appropriate care.It is free and open to all as it provides a safe and confidential environment for people to get tested and receive the care they need.

Eye Clinic 1st Wednesday of the Month

We provide services such as comprehensive eye exams, diagnosis and treatment of eye diseases and disorders, management of vision problems, prescription of eyeglasses and contact lenses, and referrals to specialists.

Inpatient Services

We offer diagnostic tests, surgery, medical treatments, and other hospital services. We have a team of healthcare professionals that includes physicians, nurses, therapists, and other specialists who work together to provide the best care possible. We also have services such as palliative care, which is focused on symptom relief and pain management.

Why we are the best

WELL EQUIPED LABORATORY

Our laboratory is typically equipped with specialized equipment and resources necessary to help diagnose, monitor, and treat diseases. These services may laboratory testing, such as blood tests, imaging tests, and genetic tests. The laboratory may also provide a range of pathology services, such as tissue analysis and biopsy. Other services may include drug testing, analysis of body fluids, and microbiology testing.

PHARMACY

Our pharmacy operates on a 24 hour basis. Our stock of medicine is thoughtfully sourced in order to handle the varying needs for both inpatient and outpatient prescriptions.

ULTRASOUND SERVICES

Ultrasound uses sound waves to create images of inside the body, which can help diagnose any abnormalities or diseases, such as tumors, cysts, gallstones, and more. Ultrasound can also be used to monitor a pregnancy and determine the fetal age and development. Ultrasound can also be used for therapeutic purposes, such as to guide biopsies and other surgical procedures.

Wards Available for Inpatient

We have quality wards with a bed capacity of 30 patients. Our hospital offers medical ward, maternity ward and also paediatric ward. In addition we have paediatric ward.

Hypertension and diabetic Clinic

Hypertension and Diabetic Clinic focuses on the care and management of patients living with hypertension (high blood pressure) and diabetes. The clinic provides comprehensive care and education to help patients manage their conditions. The goal of the clinic is to help patients reduce their risk of developing complications related to hypertension and diabetes by improving their overall health.

NHIF Accredited

We offer comprehensive covers and also other NHIF schemes such as Linda Mama and NHIF Edu Afya to our clients. We laso accept insurance for civil servants and also NHIF inpatient cover

About Us

Located in Matuu town near Matuu Parish station in Machakos county, Matuu mission Hospital is a modern 30-bed facility that is home to highly skilled and experienced and professional medical staff. For generations, we have been ranked among the best mission hospitals in Machakos county because of our excellent standards and the high quality of services and personal care we provide. We are pacesetters in our field, inspired by our core value of care. We care about the dignity of our patients and strive to become a one-stop healthcare provider in Kenya.
